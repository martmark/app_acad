Pasta Carbonara Recipe

Description

Pasta Carbonara is an Italian pasta dish from Rome made with egg, hard cheese, guanciale (or pancetta), and pepper.

Directions
  1. Boil water and add spaghetti.
  2. Cook Guanciale until crispy
  3. Add spaghetti to Guanciale pan
  4. Mix in egg yolk and grated cheese
  5. Season with black pepper
